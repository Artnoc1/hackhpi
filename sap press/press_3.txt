SAP Announces First Quarter 2016 Results
April 20, 2016 by SAP News
Cloud Revenue Up 33%; IFRS EPS Up 38%; Non-IFRS EPS Up 9%  Non-IFRS cloud and software revenue increased 5% to €3.85 billion (6% at constant currencies) SAP S/4HANA momentum continues, now exceeding 3,200 customers Growing operating profit amidst industry transformation in contrast to main peer High visibility into strong second quarter and full year pipeline across all regions indicates increasing momentum as the year progresses – SAP firmly reiterates outlook  WALLDORF, Germany — SAP SE (NYSE: SAP) today announced its financial results for the first quarter ended March 31, 2016.
