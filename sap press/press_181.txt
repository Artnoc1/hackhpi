SAP to Announce Third Quarter 2015 Earnings
October 12, 2015 by SAP News
SAP SE (NYSE: SAP) will release its results for the third quarter of 2015 on Tuesday, October 20. Bill McDermott, CEO of SAP SE, and Luka Mucic, CFO and COO of SAP SE, will host a financial analyst and press conference call to review third quarter results. Media representatives may also listen in on the Financial Analyst and Press Conference Call via Webcast at 2:00 p.m. CET / 8:00 a.m. EDT accessible here.
