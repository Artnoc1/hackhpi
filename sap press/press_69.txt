SAP to Announce Results for the First Quarter of 2015
April 13, 2015 by SAP News
SAP SE (NYSE: SAP) will release its results for the first quarter of 2015 on Tuesday, April 21. Bill McDermott, CEO of SAP SE, and Luka Mucic, CFO of SAP SE, will host a financial analyst and press conference call to review the first quarter results. Media representatives may also listen in on the financial analyst and press conference call via webcast at 2:00 p.m. CET / 8:00 a.m. EDT, accessible here.
