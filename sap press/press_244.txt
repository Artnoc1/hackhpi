January 12, 2016 by SAP News
WALLDORF — SAP SE (NYSE: SAP) will release its results for the fourth quarter of 2015 on Friday, January 22, 2016. At a press conference, the company will present fourth quarter financial figures, financial year 2015 year-end results, as well as an outlook on the current financial year. The speakers are:  Bill McDermott, CEO of SAP Luka Mucic, CFO and COO of SAP Bernd Leukert, Member of the Executive Board of SAP, responsible for Products and Innovation Steve Singh, Member of the Global Managing Board of SAP and CEO of Concur Robert Enslin, Member of the Executive Board of SAP, responsible for the Global Customer Organization  Media representatives may also listen in on the press conference via Webcast at 10:00 a.m. CET / 4:00 a.m. ET accessible here. On Monday, January 11, 2016, SAP announced preliminary results; read the press release here.
